Motivated

in a Month

It’s a familiar, discouraging cycle: decide to “get healthy”, stick with your workout or diet for a few days, then slide back into old habits. Are you ready to try something different and finally achieve your goal? It’s all about motivation!

Our Motivated in a Month program will help you identify and unlock your inner motivation— all in just 30 days. Before you know it, you’ll be setting and reaching your goals.

Yes! Motivated in a Month is for anyone who struggles to stay motivated and follow through. You see, it’s not about finding the right diet, the right workout, or the perfect schedule. Those are all external motivators, which means you’re depending on variables outside of your control.

Motivated in a Month is for people who are ready to admit and commit to doing the work on themselves—to discovering the internal motivators and adopting a growth mindset. The good news? You’ll be depending on variables inside your control. And if there’s one thing we know, it’s that you should always bet on yourself.
